Our Story

Born from a Love of Kerala's Waters

Parudeesa — meaning "paradise" — was born from a deep love of Kerala's serene backwaters and a vision to share that peace with the world. Nestled on the shores of a tranquil lake, we offer two distinct properties that blend natural beauty with thoughtful luxury.

From intimate couples' retreats to grand 200-person celebrations, every experience at Parudeesa is crafted with warmth, care, and an eye for the extraordinary. We don't just host events — we create lifelong memories.
